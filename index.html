About Us

Cloud guitars are each uniquely made with a dream sound and feel in mind. They feature some of the finest materials you'll find on the market, and are made entirely from scratch in our workshop. Our pickups are scatter-wound to sonically fit each instrument to musical taste. Our necks are hand-carved to make playability smooth so the player can glide cleanly across the fretboard, or stretch to grip that tough chord that seemed just out of reach. Every guitar made at Cloud Guitars is a high-end custom guitar suitable for the modern player.
